Process

Three steps to a
living assistant

Like any organism, your AI assistant grows from what you feed it — your documents, your voice, your knowledge.

01

Feed it knowledge

Upload your documents, FAQs, guides, and processes. OrgMind digests them and makes them searchable in natural conversation.

02

Shape its voice

Give your assistant a name, a personality, a purpose. Define how it speaks, what it knows, and where its boundaries are.

03

Let it grow

Deploy to your team or customers. Your assistant learns from every interaction, getting sharper and more useful over time.
